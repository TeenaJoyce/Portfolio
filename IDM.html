IDM Logo Redesign Project

Trinity College Dublin – MSc in Interactive Digital Media

We redesigned the logo for the IDM course, exploring its identity through research, surveys, and branding strategy.

Research Phase

Initial Research

  • Reviewed logos of institutions focused on tech, creativity, and innovation.
  • Studied notable rebrands — MIT Media Lab (successful), Pepsi (unsuccessful).

Survey

  • Google Form with 30 participants from the IDM WhatsApp group.
  • Questions included:
    • “What word best describes IDM?”
    • “Does the course name reflect the curriculum?”
    • “What attracted you to the course?”

Findings

  • IDM is widely associated with technology and creativity.
  • The course name was generally seen as accurate.
  • Popular among students from Ireland, China, and India.
  • Most enrolled shortly after undergrad.
Personas
  • Based on survey data, we created fictional personas.
  • Represented tech-savvy, globally minded students seeking creative careers.
Design Strategy

Visual Identity

  • Brand direction leaned into technology themes.
  • Supported by survey insights and course module content.

Naming

  • The name “Interactive Digital Media” was retained.
  • Changing it could risk misalignment with audience perception.
