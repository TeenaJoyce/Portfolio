About the Project

Luna is a bold and adventurous chips brand inspired by the mystique of the moon and the rich flavors of Chinese cuisine. Blending tradition with modern snacking trends, Luna offers a unique taste journey through ingredients like soy sauce, chili flakes, and aromatic spices — creating an unforgettable experience with every bite.

Vision
    Luna aims to redefine snack time by offering flavors that celebrate the depth and complexity of Chinese culinary traditions. We want to transport our customers to a world of vibrant tastes, bringing global flavors to everyday moments with authenticity, creativity, and a spirit of discovery.
Research Phase
    Our research involved studying current snack food trends, the rising interest in global and fusion flavors, and the emotional appeal of cultural storytelling. We also examined packaging styles, color psychology, and successful branding strategies used by leading snack brands worldwide.
Aesthetics
    Luna’s aesthetic combines mystical, moonlit themes with bold, rich colors inspired by Chinese culture — think deep blues, glowing golds, and fiery reds. The visual style balances modern minimalism with traditional motifs, creating a design that feels both contemporary and rooted in heritage.
Personas
  • The Flavor Explorer: A foodie who loves trying new, global tastes and seeks out unique snacking experiences.
  • The Trendsetter: A young adult who values design, aesthetic, and innovative brands that feel authentic and fresh
  • The Comfort Seeker: Someone who enjoys nostalgic, comforting flavors but is open to bold twists on classics.
